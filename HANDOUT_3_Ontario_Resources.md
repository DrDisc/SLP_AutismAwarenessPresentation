## Financial Support & Funding

### 1. Registered Disability Savings Plan (RDSP)
**What:** Long-term savings plan for people with disabilities
**Benefits:**
- Government matching grants
- Tax-deferred growth
- Government bonds for low-income families

**Info:** ğŸŒ www.canada.ca/rdsp

---

### 2. Child Disability Benefit
**What:** Tax-free monthly payment for families caring for child with disability
**Eligibility:** Must qualify for Disability Tax Credit (DTC)

**Info:** ğŸ“ 1-800-387-1193 or www.canada.ca

---

### 3. Disability Tax Credit (DTC)
**What:** Non-refundable tax credit
**How:** Doctor completes Form T2201
**Benefits:** Can open access to other programs (RDSP, Child Disability Benefit)

**Info:** ğŸ“ 1-800-959-8281

---

### 4. Special Services at Home (SSAH)
**What:** Financial support for services at home
**Who:** Children with developmental and/or physical disabilities
**Use:** Respite care, specialized equipment, professional services

**Access:** Through your local children's treatment centre

---

### 5. Assistance for Children with Severe Disabilities (ACSD)
**What:** Monthly payment to help with extraordinary costs
**Eligibility:** Child under 18 with severe disability

## Checklist: Steps to Take After Diagnosis

**Immediate (First Month):**
- [ ] Register with Ontario Autism Program
- [ ] Contact local Children's Treatment Centre
- [ ] Book appointment with pediatrician/family doctor
- [ ] Apply for Disability Tax Credit (Form T2201)
- [ ] Connect with Autism Ontario local chapter
- [ ] Research preschool speech & language programs (if under 5)
- [ ] Contact school for IEP meeting (if school-age)

**Short-Term (1-3 Months):**
- [ ] Start speech-language services
- [ ] Join parent support group
- [ ] Attend parent training workshop
- [ ] Apply for Special Services at Home (SSAH)
- [ ] Read recommended books
- [ ] Connect with other families

**Ongoing:**
- [ ] Attend all therapy appointments
- [ ] Participate in IEP meetings
- [ ] Implement home strategies
- [ ] Celebrate progress
- [ ] Practice self-care
- [ ] Advocate for your child
