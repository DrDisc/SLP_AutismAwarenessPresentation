## ü§î FAQ

**Q: Do I need to download ALL suggested images?**
A: No! The guides suggest 5-7 options per category. Pick 3-5 that resonate with you.

**Q: Can I mix professional and playful on the same handout?**
A: Yes! In fact, it's recommended. See `CHOOSING_ARTWORK_STYLES.md` for guidance.

**Q: Do I need to give credit/attribution?**
A: Most free sources don't require it, but it's nice to give credit. Check each platform's requirements.

**Q: What resolution should images be?**
A: Minimum 1920x1080px for best quality in print/digital handouts.

**Q: Can I edit/modify images?**
A: Yes, all free sources allow it. Just maintain the license terms.

**Q: Where should I save the images?**
A: Use the `_playful` and non-playful subfolders based on image type.

**Q: Can I use images from other sources?**
A: Yes, but verify they're free to use and properly licensed.
