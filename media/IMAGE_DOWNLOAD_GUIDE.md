## How to Download Images

### Step-by-Step Instructions:

1. **Go to the URL** provided for your search query
2. **Browse the results** and select an image that appeals to you
3. **Click on the image** to open the full preview
4. **Look for the Download button**
   - Unsplash: Green "Download Free" button
   - Pexels: "Download" button in the corner
   - Pixabay: "Download" button at the top
5. **Choose your resolution**
   - Recommended: 1920x1080px or higher for best quality
6. **Save the file** to the appropriate folder:
   - Handout 1 images → `media/handout_1_slp_info/`
   - Handout 2 images → `media/handout_2_communication_strategies/`
   - Handout 3 images → `media/handout_3_ontario_resources/`
7. **Rename the file** using the suggested name from this guide

## Image Quality Checklist

When selecting images, verify:

- ✅ **Diversity:** Multiple ethnicities, family structures, abilities represented
- ✅ **Authenticity:** Real photos, not overly staged or generic stock photos
- ✅ **Relevance:** Directly connects to handout theme
- ✅ **Quality:** Clear, well-lit, professional appearance
- ✅ **Composition:** Works well for print and digital formats
- ✅ **Resolution:** At least 1920x1080px for handouts
- ✅ **Tone:** Positive, supportive, professional

## Tips for Best Results

### Search Tips:
- Try multiple search terms to find the right image
- Use specific terms like "diverse family" rather than just "family"
- Sort by "popular" to see highest-quality images first
- Look at photographer profiles for collections of related images

### Diversity Tips:
- Search with diversity keywords: "diverse," "multicultural," "inclusive"
- Check that children/families represent various ethnicities
- Look for images showing different abilities or family structures
- Avoid images that only show one type of family

### Design Tips:
- Choose images with good color balance for your document layout
- Consider image orientation (landscape vs. portrait) for your handout
- Ensure text overlay will be readable if you plan to add captions
- Test how images look when resized to handout dimensions
